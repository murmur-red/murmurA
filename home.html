CS Roadmap

08/2025

  • Onboarding and Assessment: Learn existing processes: Review current customer success workflows and tools
  • Cross-Team Collaboration: Meet with Product, Sales, and Development teams to understand the broader strategy.
  • Identify Strengths and Gaps: Determine what's working well and what needs improvement.

09/2025

  • Process Optimization and Quick Wins: Refine Workflows: Implement improvements in client onboarding and support processes.
  • Client Mapping: Develop personalized strategies for different client segments to ensure their unique needs are met. (CS Playbook Version 0.1)
  • Sales Feedback Loop: Ensure Sales provides key details about each client, like developer resources and design team involvement (etc).
  • Data Monitoring: Build a health scoring system using SDK integration status, transaction volume, user engagement and retention, support signals (Slack messages or tickets).

10/2025

  • Proactive Client Touchpoints: Schedule regular check-ins and business reviews with key clients to surface risks and upsell opportunities early (new titles).
  • CRM: Choose and implement tools for client management and success tracking.
  • Feedback Loop: Create a system for regular updates from clients and internal teams on new developments.
  • Internal Enablement: Train Sales and Product teams on customer insights gathered so far to refine pre-sale and post-sale alignment

11/2025

  • Proactive Engagement and Metrics: Client Success Metric: Set clear KPIs for customer health, onboarding speed, support responsiveness, and expansion potential
  • Upsell: Define clear signals for upsell and cross-sell moments.
  • CS Playbook Version 0.2: Update the client mapping and onboarding playbook based on learnings, adding edge-case handling and escalation paths.
  • Data Consolidation: Integrate client feedback into a central dashboard for visibility on trends and actionable items
  • Customer Advocacy Program: Identify satisfied customers and invite them for testimonials, case studies, or speaking opportunities

12/2025

  • Scaling: Automation Opportunities: Identify repeatable manual tasks (follow-ups, status updates, onboarding steps) that can be automated or AI-ed to save time.
  • Team Growth Plan: Assess workload vs pipeline and draft a resource plan for when to hire additional CS resources
  • Proactive Risk Management: Build a simple system for flagging accounts at risk (low usage, support signals) and define clear intervention steps
